Generally useful utility classes. As always, the utility classes are the most important ones in the whole project, so I recommend reading their documentation before modifying the code.

The time- and date-related methods of the {@link salssuite.util.Util} class are especially important because they can be used in databases to provide a standardised date and time format. @see salssuite.util.Util#getTimeString @see salssuite.util.Util#parseTimeString @see salssuite.util.Util#getDateString @see salssuite.util.Util#parseDateString

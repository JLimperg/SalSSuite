Classes related to the magazine client.

The JFrame MagazineClient and its MagazineClientXXXPanels make up the magazine client for the SalSSuite. The two dialogs are used for special sorts of user interaction.

The magazine client is divided up into a main frame (MagazineClient) and three top-level panels (MagazineClientOrdersPanel; MagazineClientShoppingListPanel; MagazineClientWarePanel), two of which in turn have sub-panels. The frame is a simple wrapper around the panels; they do the actual interaction with the user.

For a general description of what can be done with the magazine client, please refer to its {@link salssuite.clients.magazine.MagazineClient documentation}. More detailed information is given in the javadoc of {@link salssuite.clients.magazine.MagazineClientOrdersPanel}, {@link salssuite.clients.magazine.MagazineClientShoppingListPanel}, and {@link salssuite.clients.magazine.MagazineClientWarePanel}.
